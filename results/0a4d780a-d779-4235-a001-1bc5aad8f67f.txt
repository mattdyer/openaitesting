pulled pork recipe

Ingredients:

-4-5 lb pork shoulder
-1/4 cup olive oil
-1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
-1/4 cup brown sugar
-2 tablespoons smoked paprika
-2 tablespoons garlic powder
-2 tablespoons onion powder
-2 tablespoons chili powder
-1 tablespoon ground cumin
-1 teaspoon ground black pepper
-1 teaspoon sea salt
-1/2 cup chicken broth
-1/2 cup BBQ sauce

Instructions:

1. Preheat oven to 300Â°F.

2. In a small bowl, mix together olive oil, apple cider vinegar, brown sugar, smoked paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, chili powder, cumin, black pepper, and sea salt.

3. Rub the mixture all over the pork shoulder.

4. Place the pork shoulder in a roasting pan and pour the chicken broth and BBQ sauce over it.

5. Cover the roasting pan with aluminum foil and bake for 3-4 hours, or until the pork is tender and easily shreds with a fork.

6. Remove the pork from the oven and shred with two forks.

7. Serve with your favorite BBQ sauce and sides. Enjoy!
